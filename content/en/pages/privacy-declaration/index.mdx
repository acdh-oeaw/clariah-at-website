Your data will be processed for grant administration and to reach out to you for subsequent discussions/questions as well as for the documentation of your research outcome or research travel (depending on which grant you applied for).

You have the right to request access to and rectification or erasure of personal data or restriction of processing or to object to processing as well as the right to data portability. Furthermore, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority in Austria or another EU member state. The Austrian supervisory authority is the [Austrian Data Protection Authority ](https://www.dsb.gv.at/).
