**About DARIAH-CAMPUS**

![](/assets/content/assets/en/events/dariah-campus-how-to-workshop/download-3.png)

DARIAH-Campus is a discovery framework and hosting platform for learning resources and one of the core services of [DARIAH](https://www.dariah.eu/). For **learners**, it provides trustworthy, reusable open learning resources; for **trainers**, it ensures impact and sustainability of training resources.
