## Digital Humanities Festival at CEU

**When:** Thursday, December 4th 2025

**Where:** Central European University (CEU) - Auditorium\
Quellenstraße 51, 1100 Vienna,&#x20;

**Registration:** Register via the [**registration form**](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdYcnviWw5m1nXElgNQNG0-ofx8ZPjhpkKw6UxY5KLYHsYbiQ/viewform) until 7.11.2025 is mandatory.

Please note that the afternoon session is open only to members of the [Cluster of Excellence EurAsian Transformations](https://www.oeaw.ac.at/eurasian-transformations/home). Please also note that the capacity is limited. Lunch will be provided to those who attend at least the morning session.

**Language:** English

---

## **Program**

**Morning session: CLARIAH-AT Roadshow**

* **Welcome greetings** by Walter Scholger ([University of Graz](/consortium/university-of-graz/), CLARIAH-AT spokesperson and National Coordinator for Austria at [DARIAH-EU](https://www.dariah.eu/)), Prof. Ágnes Bátory (Pro-Rector for Research and Faculty and Professor of Public Policy at [CEU](/consortium/central-european-university/)), [Dr. Alíz Horváth](/consortium/central-european-university/) (Main organizer, Assistant Professor of East Asian History and Digital Humanities at CEU, CEU representative at CLARIAH-AT)
* **CEU Project Medley** / DH at CEU - CEU in DH: Digitally-enhanced project showcase by CEU faculty and students
* **CLARIAH-AT Project Showcase:** DH project presentations by CLARIAH-AT partner institutions from around Austria

**Afternoon session: Bring your project ideas - match them with digital tools** 

Practical workshop-style consultation session (a form of "project incubation") with the [Austrian Centre for Digital Humanities (ACDH),](/consortium/austrian-academy-of-sciences/) followed by a collective group discussion on how these projects could fit into the larger ecosystem of the Cluster's Digital Lab.

The afternoon event is geared towards members of the [Cluster of Excellence EurAsian Transformations](https://www.oeaw.ac.at/eurasian-transformations/home). If you would like to attend this part of the event, please make sure to fill out the relevant questionnaire in [this form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdYcnviWw5m1nXElgNQNG0-ofx8ZPjhpkKw6UxY5KLYHsYbiQ/viewform) to the best of your knowledge. This will make it easier for the ACDH to help you effectively. Thank you for your cooperation. (Please note that the afternoon session will be an in-person only event.)
