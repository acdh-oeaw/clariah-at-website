## **Sprache/Macht/Daten – Digitales Kulturerbe öffnen und vernetzen**

**When:** Tuesday, May 5th - Wednesday, May 6th, 2026

**Where:** kärnten.museum\
Museumgasse 2, 9021 Klagenfurt am Wörthersee

**Registration:** Please register in advance for this event via [this registration form](https://mailworx.marketingsuite.info/Form?frm=b0451b5b-75c5-4757-9156-ea827cadc299).&#x20;

---

Under the theme  **„Sprache/Macht/Daten – Digitales Kulturerbe öffnen und vernetzen“** (**“Language/Power/Data – Opening up and Connecting Digital Cultural Heritage”)**, we warmly invite you to the **Stakeholder Forum 2026**:

The focus is on how interconnected knowledge can unleash its transformative power – for research, education and society.

Participants may look forward to three high-profile keynote speeches:

* **Sarah Kenderdine** (EPFL Lausanne) will demonstrate how museums are using new technologies such as VR, AI and data visualisation responsibly.
* **Clemens Apprich** (die Angewandte) will take a critical look at how digital infrastructures shape power relations and cultural practices.
* **Sören Auer** (TIB Hannover) explains how **Linked Open Data** sustainably connects and makes cultural heritage accessible.

The programme is complemented by numerous [Lightning Talks](https://clariah.at/en/news/stakeholder-forum-2026-call-for-interest/) from the Kulturpool Community, workshops and discussion forums. Together we will discuss:

* How can cultural institutions fulfil their responsibilities in the digital sphere?
* What data and infrastructure are needed for sustainable networking?
* And how can digital resources be used responsibly?
