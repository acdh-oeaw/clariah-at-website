**When:** Monday, January 20 - Friday, January 24, 2025

**Where:** Austrian Academy of Sciences, Campus Akademie, \
Bäckerstraße 13, 1010 Vienna\
Seminarraum 1

**Registration:** You can register for the ÖAW AI Winter School via their [website](https://indico.cern.ch/event/1482877/) until 10.1.2025.

---

The 4th AI winter school of the Austrian Academy of Sciences (ÖAW) and the Machine Learning Topical Platform MLA2S, is held from January 20 – 24, 2025 in Vienna.

The topics of this installment are:

* Data Handling and Processing
* Natural Language Processing and Large Language Models
* Geometric Deep Learning

The school is open to people affiliated with or working closely with institutes of the Austrian Academy of Sciences. All levels of seniority are welcome: students (MSc, PhD), PostDocs, faculty and staff.

Local organizers:

* Claudius Krause (HEPHY)
* Nicki Holighaus (ARI)
* Markus Kunzmann (ACDH-CH)
* Brigitte DeMonte (HEPHY, secretary)
