## Lecture Series at  the University of Vienna

### Summer Semester 2026

**When:** weekly every Tuesday from 10.3.2026, 16:45 - 18:15

**Where:** University of Vienna, Hauptgebäude, Hörsaal 2, Tiefparterre, Stiege 5

**Registration:** Not required.

**Language:** English and German

---

The department for Digital Philology cordially invites you to the lecture series ['Introduction to Digital Humanities'](https://evsl.univie.ac.at/digitale-philologie/veranstaltungen-vortraege-kurse/introduction-to-digital-humanities-lecture-series/):

The goal of the lecture series is to provide an overview of the state of research and current theory, practice and methods in the digital humanities. Speakers from different areas of the humanities will show how the digital approaches they use open up new possibilities for their research.
