Workshop on Early Modern Book History-related Data & Tools

**When**: 11 January 2023, 10:00 – 15:00

**Where**: online via [Zoom](https://univienna.zoom.us/j/67159129347?pwd=OHhsOUNVQzFjcHIvdVMvRkVsclREdz09#success) // Austrian National Library (ÖNB), Vienna

**Registration**: –

The network Central European Book History (Thomas Wallnig) together with the Austrian National Library (ÖNB, Vienna) organizes a workshop on early modern book history-related data & tools, on Wednesday, 11 January 2023, 10am-3pm CET.

Speakers will include Stefan Benz, Mona Garloff, Péter Király, Martin Krickl, Róbert Péter and others.

**If you have data or tools to share yourself, or if you are just interested to listen, you are welcome to participate online!** (see Zoom-link above.)
