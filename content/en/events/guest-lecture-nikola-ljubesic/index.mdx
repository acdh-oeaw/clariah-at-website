## Multilingual NLP can shed light on many secrets of parliamentary proceedings

**When:** Monday, 19th January 2026, at 1:15 pm

**Where:** University of Vienna

**Organisation:** [University of Vienna](/consortium/university-of-vienna/), in cooperation with the [Austrian Centre for Digital Humanities](/consortium/austrian-academy-of-sciences/) of the Austrian Academy of Sciences

---

Guest lecture by Nikola Ljubešić from the Jožef Stefan Institute (Ljubljana, Slovenia) on "Multilingual NLP can shed light on many secrets of parliamentary Proceedings" in the University Course [Applied NLP Tools for Digital Humanities at the University of Vienna](https://ufind.univie.ac.at/de/course.html?lv=136040\&semester=2025W), in cooperation with the Austrian Centre for Digital Humanities of the Austrian Academy of Sciences on 19th January 2026 on 13:15.

Nikola will talk about the [ParlaMint](https://www.clarin.eu/parlamint) dataset, multilingual comparable and interoperable pariamentary corpora, as well as the application of multilingual NLP in the [ParlaCAP](https://oscars-project.eu/projects/parlacap-comparing-agenda-settings-across-parliaments-parlamint-dataset) and [ParlaSent](https://aclanthology.org/2024.lrec-main.1393.pdf) Project.&#x20;

Finally, he will touch also upon [CLARIN](https://www.clarin.eu/), [CLARIN.si](https://www.clarin.si/info/about/) and the CLARIN K-Centre [CLASSLA](https://www.clarin.eu/k-centres/classla).
