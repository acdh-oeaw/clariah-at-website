The [ONB Labs](https://labs.onb.ac.at/) are offering the opportunity for a **two-month internship** as part of a holiday position at the Research and Data Services Department of the Austrian National Library.

Are you studying digital art, media art, media technology or design or a similar field of study and would like to create a digital artwork for the [ONB Labs Artspace](https://labs.onb.ac.at/artspace/) based on the digital holdings of the Austrian National Library as part of a two-month internship (holiday position of 20 hours/week) in the **period between August 1 and September 30, 2025**?

Do you already have some experience in the production of digital artworks? Then the ÖNB Labs Team is looking forward to receiving your **application** (CV, motivation incl. brief description of your idea) **by Sunday, June 22, 2025** via the Job-Portal of the Austrian National Library:

<LinkButton label="Job portal ÖNB" link={{"discriminant":"external","value":"https://www.onb.ac.at/mehr/karriere/offene-stellen"}} />

If you have any questions, please reach out to the ÖNB Labs team at [labs /at/ onb.ac.at](mailto:labs@onb.ac.at).
