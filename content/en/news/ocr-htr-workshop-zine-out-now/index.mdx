## Ten annoying things about digitizing under-resourced and under-represented languages - And what might help fixing them

We are pleased to announce that the outcomes of the writing sprint during the Workshop [OCR/HTR for Under Researched and Under Represented Languages in DH](/events/ocr-htr-workshop/), held at the Central European University in Vienna from 3 - 4.10.2025, are now available online.

Contributors (A-Z): Olaf Berg, Colin Brisson, Grigor Boykov, Saranya Chandran, Dimitra Grigoriou, Wayne de Fremery, Rachael Griffiths, Alíz Horváth, Johannes Knüchel, Yavuz Köse, Stephan Kurz, Bruno Lainé, Patrick McAllister, Nicole Merkel-Hilf, Jan Odstrčilík, Alexander O’Neill, Helmut Reimitz, Sinai Rusinek, Andy Stauder, Christoph Steindl, Markus Viehbeck.
