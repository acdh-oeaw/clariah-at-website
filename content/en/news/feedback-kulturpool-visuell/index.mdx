Researchers at the University for Continuing Education Krems have developed an exploratory interface for the national cultural heritage: they aim to create an alternative way of accessing the data from [kulturpool.at](https://www.kulturpool.at) – and the team relies on your feedback to further develop the interface.

<LinkButton label="Feedback zur Anwendung" link={{"discriminant":"external","value":"https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdk6BfTEFMoXOhIZStfbP9PmhNmInKJvanfzmZuyy7-LvpEcw/viewform"}} />

The research project Zukunft Kulturpool Visuell (*Future Kulturpool Visual)* is carried out by the University for Continuing Education Krems and funded by CLARIAH-AT.
