exploreAT! was a 5 year project carried out in collaboration with Adapt Centre and the University of Salamanca, chaired by exploration space at the Austrian Academy of Sciences.

exploreAT! designed, prototyped and implemented the frist Open Innotation Research Infrastucture (OI-RI) and run the first demonstrator on cultural knowledge co-creation and applied humanities. exploration space is now a best practice for Open Innovation of the Austrian Ministry and is an ongoing research project of Eveline Wandl-Vogt.
