With a selection of its extensive holdings featuring European Romani culture the Phonogrammarchiv contributed to the web exhibition "[A Roma Journey](http://www.theeuropeanlibrary.org/exhibition/roma_journey/eng/audio.html)" of The European Library in 2010. Together with a more detailed documentation and English translations the recordings are also accessible via the Phonogrammarchiv's [online catalogue](http://catalog.pha.oeaw.ac.at/ui/miha/sessions.php?gList_1=ethnien-ethnie&gWert_1=152&gList_2_op=undnicht&gList_2_opstrict=on&gList_2=projekt&gWert_2Eingabe=radio+r&gWert_2=378&untergruppen=J&synonyme=J&onlinemedia=ja).
