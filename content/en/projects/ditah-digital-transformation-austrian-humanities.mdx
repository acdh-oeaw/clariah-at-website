This project aims at the establishment of methods and approaches developed in the field of Digital Humanities in everyday Humanities scholarship and education. For this purpose, solutions will be created jointly in three main areas: Integration of digital infrastructures, low-threshold access to digital methods and tools, and the mediation of digital literacy in research and education. In the project consortium major Austrian DH institutions are represented.

*   In cooperation with: Akademie der bildenden Künste Wien, Donau-Universität Krems, Österreichische Akademie der Wissenschaften, Österreichische Nationalbibliothek, Paris-Lodron-Universität Salzburg, Universität Graz (Projektkoordination), Universität Innsbruck, Universität Wien, Technische Universität Wien
*   Funding: BMBWF „Digitale und soziale Transformation in der Hochschulbildung“
