In 2013, "Saving Our Heritage for the Next Generation" was the motto of UNESCO's "World Day for Audiovisual Heritage" (27 October). On that occasion, the Phonogrammarchiv set up an [online exhibition](http://www.phonogrammarchiv.at/wwwnew/ausstellung_e.htm) to provide insights into its unique and manifold holdings. Access the Phonogrammarchiv's [catalogue](http://catalog.phonogrammarchiv.at/ui/miha/sessions.php?nurSuchformAktualisieren=&suchformAnker=&gList_1=&sortieren=signatur&untergruppen=J&synonyme=J&onlinemedia=ja) to hear more of its holdings online.
