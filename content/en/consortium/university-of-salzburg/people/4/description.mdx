Ingrid Matschinegg is a Senior Scientist at the [Institute of Medieval and Early Modern Material Culture](https://www.imareal.sbg.ac.at/) where she works on the development and implementation of various Digital Humanities projects. Her main research focus is on the digital indexing of text and image sources, as well as on the development of digital tools for computer-assisted analysis. She also covers these areas in university teaching at the University of Salzburg.
