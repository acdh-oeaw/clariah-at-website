Günter Mühlberger is the initiator of the [Transkirbus](https://readcoop.eu/transkribus/) platform.

He is head of the Digitization and Digital Preservation group at the University of Innsbruck and head of FZDH. \
Additionally, he is Chairman of the Board of Directors of the European Cooperative Society [READ-COOP SCE](https://readcoop.eu/).

He studied German philology, philosophy, psychology and education at the University of Innsbruck.
